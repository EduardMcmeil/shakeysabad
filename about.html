The Story of Shakey’s Philippines

Shakey’s was founded in 1954 in California, but it was introduced to the Philippines in 1975. Since then, Shakey’s Philippines has become a household name, known for its iconic thin-crust pizzas, Chicken ’n Mojos, and friendly family dining experience. Operated by Shakey’s Pizza Asia Ventures Inc. (SPAVI), it has expanded to hundreds of stores nationwide with a strong delivery and online presence.
